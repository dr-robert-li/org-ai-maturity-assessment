## Tips for Effective Review

1. **Check facts first** — verify any numbers, dates, names, or claims against primary sources
2. **Read as the audience** — would the intended recipient find this accurate and useful?
3. **Look for confident wrongness** — Claude can state incorrect things very convincingly
4. **Check for omissions** — what should be there but isn't?
5. **Verify structure** — does the format match what was requested?
