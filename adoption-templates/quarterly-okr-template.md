# Quarterly OKR Template for AI Adoption

> **Purpose**: Set and track Objectives and Key Results for AI adoption initiatives. Use one OKR per strategic objective. Review weekly, score at quarter-end.
>
> **Source**: Adapted from [Emerging Practices in Team Building for an Agentic Internet](https://drli.blog/posts/emerging-practices-3t-team/)
>
> **Scoring note**: A score of 0.7–0.8 is the target range. Consistently scoring 1.0 means OKRs are not ambitious enough.
