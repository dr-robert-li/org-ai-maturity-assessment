## Agenda

| Segment | Duration | Activities |
|---------|:--------:|------------|
| Context | 5 min | Sprint goal reminder, key metrics from last sprint |
| Demonstrations | 25 min | 5 stories × 5 min each (see format below) |
| Q&A and Feedback | 10 min | Gather overall impressions and concerns |
| Next Sprint Preview | 5 min | Preview upcoming priorities |

**Total: 45 minutes**

---

## Demonstration Format (per story)

For each item demonstrated, cover:

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Story / Feature | _What was built or improved_ |
| Acceptance Criteria | _What it needed to do_ |
| Live Demo | _Show it working — focus on user value, not implementation_ |
| Q&A | _2 minutes for questions_ |
