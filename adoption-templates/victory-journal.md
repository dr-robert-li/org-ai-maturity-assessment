# Victory Journal Templates

> **Purpose**: Track wins — big and small — from AI adoption. Builds momentum, surfaces patterns, and creates a record of value delivered. Use the daily template individually and the weekly template with your team.
>
> **Source**: Adapted from [Emerging Practices in Team Building for an Agentic Internet](https://drli.blog/posts/emerging-practices-3t-team/)

## Weekly Victory Sharing Circle (30 min)

Use this format for a team session — ideally the same time each week.

| Segment | Duration | Activities |
|---------|:--------:|------------|
| Opening | 2 min | Set purpose and context |
| Victory Sharing | 20 min | Each person shares 1–2 victories with brief context |
| Team Celebration | 5 min | Recognise patterns and collective wins |
| Photo for Victory Wall | 3 min | Capture the moment (optional but recommended) |

### Victory Sharing Prompts

Use these to help people articulate their wins:

- "I used Claude to ______ and it saved me ______ time"
- "I discovered that Claude can ______ which helped with ______"
- "I shared a prompt with ______ and they were able to ______"
- "I caught Claude making a mistake when ______ and corrected it by ______"
- "The most surprising thing Claude did this week was ______"
